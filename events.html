MY FAVORITE

I love all of the events at Mount Zion, but my favorite has to be the High School Spring Formal. The first one happened my freshman year, and I just fell in love with it. I ended up planning it every year after that as a part of Student Council. Everyone has a great time getting to dress up, dance, and have dinner together. Each year has a different theme, and they can get pretty creative. Some of my best memories were made during Spring Formal.

Alexus Lefort


Spring Formal may be my favorite, but in a close second is the Winter Soiree. It is similar to Spring Formal, except it is a semi-formal and it is held off campus. I only got to attend one, since is started in my senior year, but that night was an absolute blast.


The Harvest Party is a great way to bring in the new year and to celebrate the cooler weather's arrival. It's a great evening of fun, music, and usually sitting around the fire. Like the Winter Soiree, the Harvest Party is held off campus; in recent years it has been held a a student's house.
