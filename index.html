ABOUT ME

Welcome!

I graduated from Mount Zion in 2016 after attending the school since 6th grade. My hope is that through this webpage you will be able to experience the school even if you have never set foot in the building. I am so glad that this is the school that my parents chose for me, even if I was not happy with the thought of going to a new school at first. Mount Zion was and still is such a blessing in my life. I made life-long friends there who have had such an impact on my life.

Alexus Lefort


Mount Zion is a small private Christian school that offers preschool through 12th grade. In recent years, it has been ranked in the top 50 Christian schools in the United States by The Best Schools, and it accredited by Association of Christian Schools International and the New England Association of Schools and Colleges. Mount Zion is also a member of the Granite State Christian Schools Association.
